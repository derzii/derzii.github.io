// bio

I'm Rezeon, 19. I write essays at the intersection of philosophy, epistemology, psychology, and everyday human behaviour. My essays try to cleanse the surface that would allow assumptions to pass unnoticed, exposing the subtle distortions that shape perception, belief, and judgment. I focus on how people come to know what they think they know.How fragile most convictions become under scrutiny. Rather than constructing new beliefs, I dismantle existing ones.

The aim is not persuasion, but precision, forcing clarity where ambiguity is often left unchallenged.

Based in Bangladesh. Writing in both English and Bangla, because some ideas translate better in the language you grew up thinking in.
